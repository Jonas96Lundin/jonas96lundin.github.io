Puzzle Platformer

Flip

A puzzle platformer game that I created together with three other students from MAU in Unity. The core mechanic is to flip the gravity to complete the levels. All assets are made by ourselves and my main focus was leveldesign, 3D-modeling and post process effects.

Game Jam

This game was done on a Game Jam during a weekend in Unity. The goal is to collect as much garbage as possible and return in to the garbage truck/ship. I spent most of the time making the UI and fixing an Audio Manager.
