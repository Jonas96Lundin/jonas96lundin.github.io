Game Jam

Magnet Magnus

This game was done on Global Game Jam during a weekend in the game engine Unity. The goal of the game is to finish the different levels with the help of a magnet that the player is holding. With a press of a button the player can switch the magnetic pole between positive and negative to jump over obstacles and kill enemies. My main focus during the project was the UI, menus and programming the camera and the saving system.

Fast paced, turn-based, co-op

Office Demons

This is a fast paced, turn-based, chaotic, couch co-op that I created together with five other students from MAU and one music student from Lunds University. The game mechanics are a mix of free movement and turn based, in combat, and the biggest enemies are your friends. We took a lot of inspiration from Magicka and Overcooked. All assets are made by ourselves and my main focus was programming the save/checkpoint system and camera functionality, creating particle effects and improve user experience with graphical effects.

Puzzle Platformer

Flip

A puzzle platformer game that I created together with three other students from MAU in Unity. The core mechanic is to flip the gravity to complete the levels. All assets are made by ourselves and my main focus was leveldesign, 3D-modeling and post process effects.

Game Jam
